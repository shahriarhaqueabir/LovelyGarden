The Main Deck is the persistent workspace. It is divided into three primary zones.

Tab A: The Tactical Grid (Center View)

This is the "Game Field" where biological simulation occurs.

Sub-Tab: Visual Layers (HUD)

Growth View: Default mode showing plant icons and stage (Seedling, Mature).

Resource View: Overlays N-P-K (Nutrient) and $H_2O$ (Hydration) status bars.

Synergy View: Draws glowing "Connection Lines" between companion plants (e.g., Green for Buffs, Red for Antagonists).

Sub-Tab: The Time Scrubber

Function: A slider to scrub from Day -30 (History) to Day +90 (Projection).

Visuals: Ghosts the future growth of plants using the GDD (Growing Degree Days) calculation.

Tab B: The Strategy Sidebar (Left)

Sub-Tab: Sowing Calendar

Function: A vertical timeline of the 200+ plants.

Logic: Filters by "Plantable in Dresden Now." Uses solid bars for outdoor and dotted for indoor windows.

Sub-Tab: The Seed Vault

Function: The master CRUD interface for your "Deep Storage."

Action: Click to "Withdraw to Pocket" (moves data to the Bottom Dock).

üéí Section 2: The Command Dock (Bottom Interface)

This area handles the "Physical" transition of assets into the garden.

Tab A: The Pocket (Active Inventory)

Functionality: A horizontal "Hand" of up to 8 seed cards.

Features: Shows "Compatibility Score" as you hover a card over a specific grid slot.

Tab B: The Tool Kit

Action: Water: Resets the evaporation timer.

Action: Fertilize: Modifies the soil N-P-K metadata for that slot.

Action: Remedy: Opens the Diagnostic Wizard if a plant shows a "Stressor" icon.
