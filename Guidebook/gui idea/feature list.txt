To maximize the "high-fidelity simulation" feel, we transition the GUI from a simple app to a Command Center. This involves layering deep data visualizations (the "Tool") over a highly interactive, tactile map (the "Game").

1. Visual Navigation & HUD (The Command Center)
The Living Map (Isometric/Grid Toggle): A high-fidelity visual representation of your garden. Plants grow in size and change color based on real-time health data.

Time-Scrubbing Timeline: A slider at the bottom of the screen. Dragging it forward "ghosts" the future state of your garden based on current growth rates and the DWD (German Weather Service) forecast.

Environmental HUD: A persistent top-bar showing:

Lichtstunden: Current daily sunlight hours for Dresden.

Bodenfeuchte: A simulated "Soil Moisture" gauge calculated from the last rain vs. current evaporation.

Frost-Countdown: Days remaining until the Eisheiligen (May 15).
