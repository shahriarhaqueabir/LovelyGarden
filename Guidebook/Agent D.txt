2. Content Strategy Rationale
The "Three Sisters" Trio: I recommend adding Sweet Corn, Pole Beans, and Zucchini Squash next. These represent the ultimate mechanical synergy:

Corn provides the trellis (Visual/Mechanical).

Beans provide nitrogen (Logic Buff).

Squash provides ground cover to prevent weeds (Logic Buff/Visual).

Water Frequency Logic: Notice that Germination stages always have a 1 day water frequency. This creates a "Newbie" gameplay loop where the user must check their fresh seeds daily, but can relax as the plants mature.
